= Jens Rehsack — Platform Architect & Technical Product Owner

[quote, Friedrich Schiller]
Simplicity is the result of maturity.

== Who I Am

I am a senior platform architect with over 30 years of experience in software
engineering, specializing in embedded and IoT systems.  My work focuses on
bridging the gap between business goals and technical implementation --
translating stakeholder requirements into robust, maintainable architectures
that deliver lasting value.

I believe in iterative, purpose-driven development: small, deliberate steps
that produce usable intermediate results rather than rushing toward a distant
endpoint.  With each iteration, the understanding of actual needs and
constraints deepens.  Simplicity, in this view, is not a shortcut -- it is the
outcome of continuous reflection, honest communication, and the ability to
distinguish the essential from the incidental.
